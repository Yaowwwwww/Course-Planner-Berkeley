Our Story

TechFounders@Berkeley, formerly known as Alpha Venture Club, is UC Berkeley's only student-led, non-profit organization dedicated exclusively to technology and venture creation. We empower enthusiastic entrepreneurs to thrive in today's dynamic startup landscape with cutting-edge solutions, create a space where startups can grow, thrive, and connect with resources that will take your mission to the next level.

How We Help You Succeed

Exclusive Access to Serial Entrepreneurs and VCs

TechFounders@Berkeley's students gain unique opportunities to engage directly with experienced entrepreneurs and venture capitalists. These one-on-one interactions allow you to learn from their successes and failures, providing insights that can accelerate your path to success.

Discovering the Perfect Co-Founder for Your Startup Journey

TechFounders@Berkeley empowers students in building startups with collaboration with corporations and investors. We connect them with like-minded students who share their passion for technology and entrepreneurship, finding potential co-founders and team members who align with their vision.

Comprehensive Courses and One-on-One Mentorship

TechFounders@Berkeley's structured courses cover essential areas like business strategy, technology, and law, providing a solid foundation for building your startup. Additionally, our one-on-one mentoring sessions give you personalized guidance to navigate the challenges you’ll face.

Why Choose Us

Focus on Real-World Impact:
(Mission)
We are the only student-led organization at UC Berkeley exclusively dedicated to technology and venture creation. Our hands-on approach ensures you gain practical skills that are immediately applicable, from coding to pitching investors.

Unique Access to Top Investors/Experts:
(Vision)
Our carefully curated network gives you unparalleled access to venture capitalists, industry leaders, and tech innovators. Through mentorship and exclusive events, we provide connections that can open doors to funding, partnerships, and career opportunities.

Commitment to Empowering Founders:
(Values)
We’re not here to just teach concepts—we’re here to help you build something meaningful. With events like Demo Days, pitch competitions, and project showcases, we ensure you leave with the experience, confidence, and network needed to succeed.
